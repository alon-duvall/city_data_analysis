  • Boston Permits Changes (2023-2024)

This heatmap shows changes in building permit activity between 2023 and 2024. Regions marked in blue indicate an increase in permits issued, while red indicates a decrease.

The map was generated using data from the "Approved Building Permits" dataset available at data.boston.gov for the years 2023 and 2024. For each year, we used Gaussian kernel density estimation to produce a continuous spatial distribution of where permits were issued.

These distributions were then scaled by the total number of permits in each year. Finally, the 2023-scaled distribution was subtracted from the 2024-scaled distribution. Positive differences (more permits in 2024) are shown in blue; negative differences (fewer permits) are shown in red.

The heatmap provides a rough indication of where construction activity is increasing, which may loosely correlate with areas undergoing gentrification. However, note some caveats: the kernel density estimation may have oversmoothed the data, and permit levels in specific regions can vary year to year, so this map should not be interpreted as a definitive indicator of long-term growth.

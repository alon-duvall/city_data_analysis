The crime incident reports (2023–2025) available on the Analyze Boston website include information such as the hour of the incident, the type of crime, and the location where it occurred.

In this section, we filter the data by crime type and time of day, then compute the entropy of the location distribution—measuring how spread out or concentrated the incidents are.

Our analysis shows that early morning drug offenses tend to be less random and more geographically concentrated, often occurring in just a few specific areas across Boston.
